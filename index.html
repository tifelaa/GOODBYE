HOW TO SAY GOODBYE IN 10 LANGUAGES

welcome to my beautiful web page everyone, on this webpage that i call beautiful, i will be sharing with you how to say goodbye in 10 languages.
we all know that it is super cool to speak in different languages but it can be hard somtimes, that why we are going to be using our simple everyday words like 'Goodbye' today

The 10 different languages that i will be sharing today are; Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, French, Chinese, Korean, Spanish, Greek, Hindi and Russian.

Lets go!!!!!

  • Yoruba: O dabò
  • Igbo:Ka omesia
  • Hausa:Sai anjima
  • French:Au revoir
  • Chinese:再见 (Zàijiàn)
  • Korean:안녕히 가세요 (Annyeonghi gaseyo)
  • Spanish:Adiós
  • Greek:Αντίο (Adío)
  • Hindi: अलविदा (Alvida)
  • Russian:До свидания (Do svidaniya)
